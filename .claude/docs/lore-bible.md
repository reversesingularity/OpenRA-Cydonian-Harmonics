## The Two Factions

### Oathbound Guardians (protagonist faction)

- Resilient, long-lived oathbound warriors; faith-enhanced infantry; hero-led
  strikes; defensive resilience and tactical combos.
- **Harmonic signature: teal-gold creation harmonics.** Visual language:
  ancient-future exosuits, patched exoskeletons, dramatic lighting, cold
  tactical blues/blacks base with teal-gold accents.
- Signature tech: sentient harmonic blades that pulse or "sing" (Bocra-class);
  support structures emitting sustaining frequencies.

### Nephilim Collective / Watcher Remnants (antagonist faction)

- Aggressive swarm/tech pressure faction: corrupted harmonics, frequency
  emitters that debuff and disrupt, heavy biomechanical units, monolith
  structures.
- **Harmonic signature: amber corruption tones.** Brutal attrition aesthetic.
- Hierarchy: Watcher Remnants supply forbidden KNOWLEDGE; Nephilim supply
  physical force. Azazel (Nephilim, son of Gadreel) is the False Prophet
  figurehead — see hard lines below.

## The Acoustic Paradigm (LOCKED)

Creation was SPOKEN into being; sound/frequency is the root physic of every
supernatural element. Guardian effects are true harmonics; Collective effects
are the same physics detuned into corruption. There is NO magic, mana, spells,
or psionics vocabulary anywhere in the game. Every ability description must
name its acoustic cause (frequency, resonance, harmonic, waveform).

## The Resonance Economy

Resonance replaces ore/credits as the strategic resource:

- **Harmonic nodes** on the map emit Resonance; they are captured/attuned, not
  mined out — control, not depletion, drives the economy.
- Guardians harvest via attunement structures (sustain-frequency collectors);
  the Collective extracts via corruption siphons that visibly detune the node
  (teal-gold shifts to amber in the node's FX).
- Objectives layer on top: defend sacred sites, activate/deactivate harmonic
  nodes, survive Operational Silence phases (economy muted for both sides).

## The Oiketerion Principle (Jude 1:6) (LOCKED)

Watchers LOST their inherent supernatural gifts when they shed their celestial
bodies (the Ma'on / Oiketerion). Gameplay consequences:

- Watcher-derived units/structures act through KNOWLEDGE and technology only —
  research auras, intel abilities, engineered weapons. Never innate power.
- Nephilim units retain hybrid PHYSICALITY (size, strength — see
  `AnakimPhysicalPresenceScaler` in engine-traits.md). This is biology, not
  miracle.
- Guardian supernatural effects are externally granted (faith-sustained
  harmonics), not innate either — sustained by support-structure frequencies.
